About Wallace Heritage Gloves

Founded on a passion for baseball and a deep respect for the craftsmanship of a well-made glove, Wallace Heritage Gloves is dedicated to extending the life and legacy of your most trusted equipment. We believe every glove has a story, and we're here to help it continue.

Our commitment to quality, meticulous attention to detail, and use of premium materials ensure that each glove we touch is returned not just relaced, but revitalized, ready for many more seasons of catches, throws, and memories.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the relacing process take?

Our typical turnaround time for a full relace is 5-7 business days from the day we receive your glove. Partial relaces may be quicker.

What types of laces do you use?

We use only professional-grade rawhide laces, known for their durability and strength. We offer a variety of standard colors.

How do I ship my glove to you?

Once you select your service, we'll provide detailed instructions on how to securely package and ship your glove to our workshop.

Do you offer a warranty on your work?

Yes, we stand by our craftsmanship. All relacing services come with a 90-day warranty on the laces installed.
